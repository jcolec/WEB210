J Cole Christensen

Associate Team Leader // Graphic Design Student // Writer & Artist

Currently, I work full time as the Associate Team Leader of the Health and Beauty department of a health food store. I have worked for the same company since 2013, and I have gained valuable skills when it comes to customer service, leadership, sales, marketing and buying.

My plan is to continue to grow in my position, while I finish my Associates Degree in Advertising and Graphic Design. From my school program I've learned so much, such as some web development and various Adobe programs.

Outside of work and school, I love to draw, paint, and write stories. I've been creating and writing my whole life, and with the skills I gain in school I hope to write, draw, and publish my own comics.
